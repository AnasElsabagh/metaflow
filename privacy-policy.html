1. Data Collection

We collect information necessary to provide our services, including name, email address, and business details. We do not sell or lease your data to third parties.

2. Security Capabilities

Sitescribes takes data security seriously. We utilize industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) for all data transmissions. Access to client codebases and databases is restricted via multi-factor authentication (MFA) and secure credential management protocols.

3. Payment Card Security

Sitescribes does not store, process, or transmit full credit card numbers on our own servers. All payments are processed through our secure third-party payment partner, Wise. Our payment partner is PCI-DSS compliant, ensuring that your sensitive cardholder data is handled with the highest level of security during transmission.

4. GDPR Compliance

For our clients in the EEA/UK, we adhere to GDPR principles regarding data transparency, the right to be forgotten, and data portability. To request a data export or deletion, please contact info@sitescribes.com.
